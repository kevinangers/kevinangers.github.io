I'm a first year Master's student at the [New York University Tandon School of Engineering](https://engineering.nyu.edu/) studying Electrical Engineering.

Previously, I graduated from the [Engineering Science](https://engsci.utoronto.ca/program/majors/robotics-engineering/) program at the [University of Toronto](https://www.utoronto.ca/), where I majored in Robotics Engineering and minored in Machine Intelligence. While at UofT, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. [Florian Shkurti](https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~florian/) (affiliations: [UofT Robotics Institute](https://robotics.utoronto.ca/), [Vector Institute](https://vectorinstitute.ai/), [Acceleration Consortium](https://acceleration.utoronto.ca/)) at the [Robot Vision and Learning (RVL) Lab](https://rvl.cs.toronto.edu/) on **generalizable end-to-end robot manipulation**.
